Oregon Outdoor Library

Overview

The idea for this site/app came to me while driving with my wife through the neighborhoods in my surrounding area. While doing so, we noticed that many neighborhoods have constructed multiple miniature outdoor library's for the nearby people to use as a free book share. Seeing this I thought about how, with my knowledge of code, I could create an app or website, that would allow the members of the communities that use these book shares, to connect with one another, as well as keep track of what each book share may have in stock.

Short Term

The immediate goals of this site are to create as accurate of a database of locations and books as possible for the communities. This along with app and website feedback will help to calibrate everything as we go along.

Long Term

In the larger picture, this neighborhood book share idea was the people's creation! So in light of that, I will aim to aid the neighborhoods in keeping this a community ran on a self-sufficient system allowing this activity to "stay as technology free as possible" while still being able to connect with others that have like-minded hobbies and gather information in a timely manner.
